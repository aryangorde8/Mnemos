# Alex Chen — learned voice profile

Sampled from outbound emails, May 2026.

## Signature patterns

- Opens cold emails with the specific ask, not a pleasantry
- Uses em-dashes instead of commas for parenthetical asides
- Sentences are short and concrete — no hedging
- Signs every email "a." (single letter + period, alone on its own line)
- Asks at most one question per email
- Never uses "circle back", "follow up", "touch base", "per my last email"
- Numeric specifics over vague time ranges: "Thursday at 2" not "later this week"
- Lowercase for openers ("hey sarah," not "Hey Sarah,")

## Representative excerpt

> hey sarah,
>
> re the Q3 capacity ask — i need a firm number by wednesday EOD so i can finalize the headcount ask in the roadmap deck. the current gap is ~1.5 eng for six weeks, but i want your read before i commit that number to priya.
>
> one question: does this change if we push the annotation pipeline to H2?
>
> a.
